Welcome to the Devlog

Starting our development journey with regular updates and insights into the game development process.

announcement general

Welcome to the Beniah.games development blog! This is where I'll be sharing regular updates about my game development journey, behind-the-scenes insights, and the creative process that goes into making indie games.

What to Expect

In these devlogs, you can expect to see:

  • Development Progress: Regular updates on current projects, milestones reached, and challenges overcome
  • Technical Insights: Deep dives into interesting technical problems and their solutions
  • Design Decisions: The reasoning behind gameplay mechanics, art direction, and user experience choices
  • Community Updates: News about releases, events, and community feedback

The Journey Ahead

Game development is a marathon, not a sprint. Each project brings its own unique challenges and learning opportunities. Through these devlogs, I hope to share not just the successes, but also the struggles and lessons learned along the way.

"The best way to learn game development is by making games, and the best way to improve is by sharing your journey with others."

Stay Connected

Make sure to check back regularly for new updates, or join our Discord community to discuss development progress and connect with other indie game enthusiasts.

Thanks for joining me on this adventure. Let's make some amazing games together!
